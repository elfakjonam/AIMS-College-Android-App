The Alliance for Innovative Management and Social Studies (AIMS) college is for global learners and managers. It is a career focus college affiliated to T.U. and a center of learning for the students of Management and Humanities. It holds a pedagogic atmosphere with utmost enthusiasm for learning. AIMS College has been established by a team of energetic, dynamic, well-known and experienced academic professionals having a long time dedication.
